The Colosseum, Rome, Italy

The Colosseum is the great oval amphitheater in the center of Rome where gladiators once fought for their life.

The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is one of the greatest architectural achievements ever recorded in history. The Great Wall translates to “long fortress.”The wall was made entirely by hand.

The Taj Mahal, India

Taj Mahal, mausoleum complex in Uttar Pradesh in northern India, built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century.

Christ the Redeemer, Brazil

Christ the Redeemer is a monumental statue located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It is the symbol of the city and, beyond that, of the whole Brazil.

Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu is a lost treasure of the 15th century, a rare citadel discovered high in the Andes mountains above the Peruvian Sacred Valley.Machu Picchu is the most well-known historic site in all of Peru.

Chichen Itza, Mexico

Deep in the Mexican state of Yucatán lies Chichen Itza, a historic Mayan city built between the 9th and 12th centuries.
